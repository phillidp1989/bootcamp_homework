About Me

My name is Dan Phillips and I am an aspiring full-stack developer. I currently work at the University of Birmingham as Head of the Curriculum Management Team. I have worked at the University since 2010 and enjoy working with data. Throughout my time at the university I have worked closely with developers in the organisation's IT Services department to collaborate in the development of new staff and student-focussed applications, making use of the curriculum data I work to maintain. It is though these interactions, as well as a burgeoning interest in coding and software development, I have decided to undertake a coding bootcamp to gain a whole set of new skills which will set me up for working in an ever-expanding sector.

I have a keen interest on back-end development as data is where my interests lie and how this data is stored and called upon to provide users of applications a user friendly presentation of this data. Despite this, I have thoroughly enjoyed learning about front-end development in the first few weeks of this bootcamp and it is nice to see the tangible outputs of front-end development.
